Privacy Policy

How we handle your data.

Last Updated: February 22nd, 2026

Pull-Up Tracker ("the App") is developed by Lauri Oherd. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

1. Overview

Pull-Up Tracker is designed with privacy in mind. Your workout data stays on your devices and is never shared with third parties. We do not collect personal information, track your usage, or display advertisements.

2. Information We Collect

a. Data You Enter

The App stores only the data you explicitly enter:

  • Pull-up entries (date, time, rep count)
  • Workout type (normal, weighted, assisted, other)
  • Optional weight and notes for each entry
  • Your preferences (default reps, appearance mode, weight unit)
  • Daily and weekly goal settings

b. What We Don't Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party analytics SDKs

3. Data Storage

a. Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you enable iCloud sync or manually export it.

b. iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled for the App, your data syncs across your Apple devices using Apple's CloudKit service. This sync is:

  • End-to-end handled by Apple
  • Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Optional — you can disable iCloud sync for this app in your device Settings

The developer never has access to your iCloud data.

4. Data Export & Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Export: You can export all your data as JSON or CSV anytime via Settings → Export Data
  • Delete Entries: You can delete individual entries from the History tab
  • Delete All Data: Uninstall the App to remove all local data
  • iCloud Data: To remove synced data, disable iCloud sync for the App or manage it in your iCloud settings

5. In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional one-time Pro upgrade via Apple's StoreKit. All purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple. We do not collect, store, or have access to any payment information such as credit card numbers or billing details. Your purchase history is managed by Apple and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

6. Siri & Shortcuts

The App supports Siri voice commands and the Shortcuts app for hands-free logging. All voice processing is handled by Apple on your device or via Apple's servers. We do not receive, store, or have access to any voice or audio data.

7. Notifications

The App can send local notifications with weekly and monthly progress summaries. These notifications are generated entirely on your device — no data is sent to any server. Notification permissions are requested only with your explicit consent and can be disabled at any time in your device Settings.

8. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following Apple services:

  • SwiftData: For local data storage on your device
  • CloudKit: For optional iCloud sync (if enabled by you)
  • WidgetKit: For home screen and lock screen widgets
  • StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases (handled by Apple)
  • AppIntents: For Siri and Shortcuts integration

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

9. Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS device encryption (when you set a passcode)
  • Apple's CloudKit encryption (for synced data)
  • No transmission to external servers (beyond Apple's iCloud if enabled)

10. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The App contains no account system, tracking, or personal data collection, so we have no way to identify users by age.
